Let Him Cook - Matcha Frappucino

Matcha is made from high quality tea leaves ground down into a fine powder. It is used in a lot of recipes and adds a mild earthy, and umami flavor. Made into a Frappucino, it makes a rich, creamy, cold drink that's as good as any cafe's! Way cheaper too!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk (You can substitute oat milk for a nice earthy taste, almond works too!)
  • 1.5 tsp fine matcha powder (You can add more if you want a stronger matcha flavor)
  • 2tsp vanilla extract or 3-4 scoops of your favorite vanilla ice cream
  • Ice (Less if you're already using ice cream)
  • Whipped Cream and sea salt(optional)

Directions

  1. Sift the matcha powder through a strainer into a bowl to avoid clumps.
  2. Pour warm water into the bowl.
  3. Whisk with a matcha whisk (A normal one is fine too!) to dissolve and introduce some air, the mixture should be a bright green.
  4. Pour in to a blender along with all the other ingredients. Add more ice/icecream if you want your frappe thicker.
  5. Blend and serve with whipped cream sprinkled with sea salt on top.
